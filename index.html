Quizzle!

Quizzle is a simple quiz game featuring 10 questions from a variety of subjects.

You will have 10 seconds to answer each question.

You can also use the following power-ups to help you:

Freeze Timer - This pauses the timer on the question you're currently on.

50/50 - This removes two wrong answers from the question, making it a fifty fifty guess.

Click below to begin the quiz - best of luck!
